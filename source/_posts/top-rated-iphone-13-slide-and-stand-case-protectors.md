##  What to Look For in an iPhone 13 Wallet Case in 2023

 The first thing to consider when choosing a wallet case for your iPhone 13 is how much it can hold. All options will allow you to carry at least one card, with some options holding up to three. However, a folio case is your best bet if you'd like to carry a couple more items like receipts or rely heavily on cash.

 Another factor that determines exactly how much you can carry in your wallet is the form factor. We've already mentioned folio cases that wrap around the entire device, but there are also minimalist wallet cases for those who want something as light as possible and rugged cases for those looking for maximum protection. What you'll want in this regard will be dependent on what you like most!

 Wireless charging compatibility is another to look into when buying a wallet case, although your options will be more limited. Most wallet cases are too thick to support wireless charging, but there are still some choices for you.

 Here are six of the best wallet cases for your iPhone 13 categorized based on storage capacity, stylishness, and ability to protect your device. The list includes everything from reasonably priced slim cases to expensive rugged covers made of almost indestructible materials.
