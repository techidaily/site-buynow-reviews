## Why Choose Pro on LinkedIn? Here's Why
 LinkedIn Premium enhances the free LinkedIn experience and offers extra benefits for professionals and businesses seeking to expand their network, discover new opportunities, and gain insights into their industry. But is it worth the price of its subscription?

## What Is LinkedIn Premium?

[LinkedIn Premium](https://premium.linkedin.com/) is a paid subscription service with advanced features unavailable in the free version. These features include InMail messaging tokens to reach out to people not in your network, detailed profile views, company insights, access to LinkedIn Learning, personalized AI suggestions, private browsing, and more. Premium is offered in different tiers such as Premium Career, Business, Sales Navigator, and Recruiter Lite, each tailored to different professional needs.

 The cost of a LinkedIn Premium annual subscription depends on the account tier you choose. The lowest-priced tier is the LinkedIn Premium Career subscription, which costs $29.99 per month or $239 annually. LinkedIn Premium Career suits most career seekers looking to get hired and professionally network in their industry.

## Are LinkedIn Premium Benefits Worth It?

 LinkedIn Premium is a worthy investment for your career or business if you have the budget.

 But remember, the decision to pay for a LinkedIn Premium subscription or go with the free LinkedIn Basic lies in the level of access you want to your professional network.

 LinkedIn Premium isn't cheap, and you must balance your needs with the features you want to take advantage of. For instance, a subscription can be a good investment for recruiters and entrepreneurs. It may not be one for someone on the cusp of a career. Before you decide, score the features with an estimate of how and how much you will put them to use for specific scenarios.
